Geographical Area of Operations

Operational Footprint

NEED Organisation operates actively to support and uplift disadvantaged populations across specifically targeted regions. Our core implementation zones include the Parvatipuram Manyam, Vizianagaram, Dr. B.R. Ambedkar Konaseema, and Srikakulam districts within the state of Andhra Pradesh. Expanding our regional footprint, our operational network in Odisha is intensely focused within the Kalahandi district.

Organisational Policies

Confidentiality

Protects sensitive information and rigorously maintains the confidentiality of all associated individuals, stakeholders, and partner organisations.

Conflict of Interest

Ensures that every structural and financial decision is made exclusively in the best interest of the organisation, without personal or financial gain.

Diversity & Inclusion

Actively promotes and sustains a culture of deep inclusivity, mutual respect, and diversity across all aspects of the organisation's work.

Financial Management

Ensures highly responsible and compliant management of all finances, including advanced budgeting, strict accounting, and transparent reporting.

Governance Policy

Clearly outlines the internal governance structure, defining specific roles, operational boundaries, and leadership responsibilities.

Human Resources

Governs the comprehensive management of all staff, carefully guiding recruitment, performance management, and equitable employee benefits.

Information Technology

Ensures the secure, ethical, and highly effective use of technology, deeply prioritizing data protection, backups, and internal cyber security.

Partnership Policy

Guides the formation of the organisation's partnerships and strategic collaborations, ensuring sustainable, mutually beneficial relationships.

Safeguarding Policy

Vigorously protects vulnerable individuals—including targeted children and at-risk adults—from any form of harm, neglect, or abuse.

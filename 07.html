Governance Framework

Structure

Board of Directors The governing body of NEED, responsible for overseeing strategic direction, financial sustainability, and structural performance monitoring.

Executive Committee Tasked with implementing the decisions of the Board and seamlessly overseeing all day-to-day operations and interventions.

Secretariat Ensures that governance processes are strictly followed and all necessary statutory documentation is maintained.

Key Roles

Secretary Leads the Board of Directors, ensuring that the board functions effectively, cohesively, and aligns with the organisation's mission.

Treasurer Bears the ultimate responsibility of overseeing the organisation's financial management, robust auditing, and transparent reporting.

Board Members Provide specialized guidance, authoritative oversight, and strategic inputs on specific areas of the organisation's vast work.

Core Principles

Transparency NEED is deeply committed to absolute transparency in its governance processes, reporting, and structural decision-making.

Accountability We hold ourselves fully accountable to stakeholders, including our members, funding partners, and the rural communities we serve.

Integrity The organisation operates with uncompromising integrity, consistently upholding the highest ethical and moral standards.

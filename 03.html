Message from the Secretary

P. Venugopala Rao
Secretary / Executive Director

Welcome to NEED Organisation. NEED was established in 1992 and officially registered in 1998 under the leadership of Mr. P. Venugopala Rao. It was founded as a compassionate response to the difficult living conditions of disadvantaged communities, aiming to restore dignity and create opportunities through dedicated service.

NEED Organisation is committed to empowering individuals and communities through education, skill development, and sustainable initiatives. We strive to bridge the gap between potential and opportunity, fostering a more equitable and prosperous society. We believe in empowering through compassion, educating through innovation, and transforming communities through collective action.


Our team works continuously to support holistic community development, especially for those deprived of basic rights and essential needs. Over the years, NEED has implemented various initiatives and made meaningful contributions across Andhra Pradesh and Odisha.

Goals

  • Empower individuals and communities by enhancing their capacity and overall well-being through education, skill development, and support.
  • Promote sustainable development by encouraging practices that benefit both people and the environment.
  • Foster innovation and entrepreneurship to support job creation and economic growth.

Objectives

  • Improve access to quality education and skill development opportunities.
  • Enhance livelihoods by supporting initiatives that ensure economic stability.
  • Build capacity by strengthening individuals, communities, and organisations.
  • Promote collaboration by fostering partnerships to achieve shared goals.

We sincerely thank our management board, funders, and partner NGOs for their continued guidance and support in making our programmes successful.

We welcome feedback and suggestions as we continue our efforts to serve underprivileged and neglected communities and create a lasting positive impact.

Sincerely,
